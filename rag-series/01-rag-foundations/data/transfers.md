# Transfers: Customer FAQ

## What is the fee for an online inter-bank transfer (BI-FAST)?
BI-FAST transfers have a flat fee of Rp 2,500 per transaction, available 24/7
with a maximum of Rp 250,000,000 per transaction.

## What is the cut-off time for same-day RTGS transfers?
RTGS same-day transfers must be submitted before 15:00 WIB on business days.
Transfers submitted after the cut-off are processed the next business day.

## Is there a daily transfer limit for mobile banking?
The default daily transfer limit via mobile banking is Rp 50,000,000. This limit
can be increased up to Rp 200,000,000 by visiting a branch with valid ID.

## What is the fee for SKN (Kliring Nasional) transfers?
SKN transfers cost Rp 5,000 per transaction. SKN operates during business hours
only (08:00 - 16:00 WIB) on business days. Funds are typically received the
same day if submitted before the cut-off.

## Can I schedule a recurring transfer?
Yes. Recurring transfers (weekly or monthly) can be set up in the mobile banking app
under Transfers > Scheduled Transfers. A minimum balance equal to the transfer amount
plus any applicable fees must be maintained on the scheduled date, otherwise the
transfer will be cancelled and a notification sent to your registered mobile number.

## What happens if I enter the wrong account number?
If a transfer is sent to the wrong account number, immediately contact customer service
at 11111-222. Recall requests must be submitted within 24 hours. Recovery is not
guaranteed and depends on the receiving bank's cooperation per Bank Indonesia Regulation
No. 23/6/PBI/2021 on payment system services.
