# Compliance and KYC: Internal Reference

## What is the maximum cash deposit without additional KYC verification?
Cash deposits up to Rp 100,000,000 per transaction do not require additional
KYC verification beyond standard account opening documents, per PPATK reporting
thresholds under Law No. 8/2010 on Anti-Money Laundering (AML).

Cash transactions above Rp 100,000,000 in a single transaction or Rp 500,000,000
in a single day are classified as Cash Transaction Reports (CTR) and must be
reported to PPATK within 14 working days.

## What triggers a Suspicious Transaction Report (STR)?
A Suspicious Transaction Report (STR / LTKM) must be filed when a transaction:
- Has no clear economic purpose or is inconsistent with the customer's profile
- Involves structuring (breaking large amounts into smaller transactions to avoid CTR thresholds)
- Is associated with a Politically Exposed Person (PEP) without adequate justification
- Matches patterns listed in PPATK's typology guidance

STRs must be submitted to PPATK within 3 working days of the suspicious transaction
being identified, per Article 23 of Law No. 8/2010.

## What are the KYC refresh requirements for existing customers?
Customer Due Diligence (CDD) must be refreshed:
- **Low-risk customers:** every 3 years
- **Medium-risk customers:** every 2 years
- **High-risk customers / PEPs:** every 1 year

Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) applies to high-risk customers, PEPs, and customers
from high-risk jurisdictions per FATF recommendations, as referenced in OJK
Regulation No. 12/POJK.01/2017 on Anti-Money Laundering Program Implementation.

## How long must customer records be retained?
Customer identification records and transaction records must be retained for a minimum
of 5 years after the business relationship ends, per OJK Regulation No. 12/POJK.01/2017.
For transaction records tied to an STR or CTR filing, retention extends to 10 years.

## What are the sanctions screening requirements?
All new customers and counterparties must be screened against:
- PPATK's designated persons and entities list
- UN Security Council consolidated sanctions list
- US OFAC SDN list (for USD-denominated transactions)

Re-screening must occur at every KYC refresh cycle and whenever a sanctions list update
is published. Matches must be escalated to Compliance within 1 business day.
