# Accounts: Customer FAQ

## What documents are required to open a savings account?
To open a standard savings account you will need:
- Valid government-issued national ID (KTP for Indonesian citizens, KITAS/KITAP for foreign nationals)
- Taxpayer Identification Number (NPWP) if applicable
- Initial deposit (minimum varies by account type, see below)
- A filled account opening form (available at branch or downloadable from the website)

For joint accounts, both account holders must be present with their respective IDs.

## What is the minimum balance for a standard savings account?
The minimum average monthly balance is Rp 50,000. Accounts falling below this threshold
for three consecutive months will be charged a Rp 5,000 monthly administration fee.
Accounts with zero balance for 12 consecutive months are subject to dormancy procedures
per OJK Regulation No. 12/POJK.01/2017.

## How do I open a joint account?
Joint account opening requires all account holders to be physically present at a branch
with valid government-issued ID. The account can be configured as:
- **AND:** all holders must sign for withdrawals
- **OR:** any single holder can transact independently

## How do I close my account?
Account closure requires a branch visit with:
- Valid government-issued ID
- Original passbook (if issued)
- Debit card for destruction
Funds are disbursed via cash or transfer to another account on the same day.
Any outstanding fees or penalties must be settled before closure.

## What interest rate does the savings account earn?
Interest rates vary by account type and are subject to change. Current rates are
published on the bank's official website and updated monthly. Interest is calculated
daily on the average closing balance and credited monthly.
