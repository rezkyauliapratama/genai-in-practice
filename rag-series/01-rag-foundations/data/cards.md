# Cards: Customer FAQ

## How do I block my card immediately if it is lost or stolen?
To block a card immediately: call 11111-222, or use the mobile banking app under
Settings > Card Management > Block Card. Confirmation is sent via registered email.
Temporary card blocking is available 24/7 through the mobile app.
Permanent block requires a branch visit or customer service call.

## Can I temporarily freeze my debit card?
Yes. A temporary freeze (also called a card lock) can be activated instantly through
the mobile banking app under Settings > Card Management > Temporary Freeze.
The card can be unfrozen at any time using the same menu. No branch visit is required
for a temporary freeze.

## How do I reset my ATM or debit card PIN?
PIN reset can be done via:
- **Mobile Banking:** Settings > Security > Change Card PIN (requires OTP verification)
- **ATM:** Select 'Change PIN' from the main menu with your current PIN
- **Branch:** Visit any branch with valid government-issued ID

For security reasons, you cannot reset a PIN by phone if your card has been reported lost.

## What is the daily ATM withdrawal limit?
The default daily ATM withdrawal limit is Rp 10,000,000. This can be adjusted up to
a maximum of Rp 25,000,000 by visiting a branch or via the mobile app with valid
identity verification.

## How do I request a new physical card?
A replacement card can be requested:
- Online via mobile banking under Settings > Card Management > Request New Card
- At any branch with valid government-issued ID
Standard delivery takes 7-14 business days. Express delivery (3-5 business days)
is available at an additional fee of Rp 50,000.
